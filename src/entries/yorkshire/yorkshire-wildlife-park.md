Yorkshire Wildlife Park opened in 2009 in an area south-east of Doncaster.  Over the years it has significantly expanded.  As well as many newer animal areas, it now has an adjoining resport, restaurant, and hotel which also service the nearby Doncaster Sheffield Airport.
