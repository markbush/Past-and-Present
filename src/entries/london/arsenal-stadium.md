Arsenal Stadium (also known as “Highbury”) was used by Arsenal FC from 1913 to 2006.  Expanding the existing stadium to improve safety and increase capacity proved impossible so a new stadium was built nearby.  Emirates Stadium became the team's new home in 2006 and is one of the largest in England.  The original site was redeveloped into an apartment complex.
