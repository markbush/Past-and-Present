The original White Hart Lane Stadium was opened in 1899 and was the home of Tottenham Hotspur until 2017.  In order to improve safety and increase capacity, a new stadium was built on the same site with work starting in 2015.  Work progressed around the old stadium, allowing it to remain open as long as possible.  The old stadium was closed in 2017 and demolished to allow the completion of the new stadium which opened in 2019.
